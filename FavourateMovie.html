Best Movies According To Utkarsh

Top three movies of all time -

Your Name

This is one of the best animated movies I have watched. The animation of this movie is on next level. Not only the animation the way they have narrated the story is also praise worthy.

Batman Trilogy

This movie series needs no introduction, it's just so good. The action, the characters and the plot are just perfect in every sense.

Bahubali

It's an Indian film and gives me goosebumps everytime I watch it. The action scenes and how they interact with music is just awesome. They have built an awesome set for this movie, I could'nt even believe Indians could do something this well.
